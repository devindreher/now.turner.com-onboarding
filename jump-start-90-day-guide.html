Your 90-day guide.


Your first 90 days with Turner will no doubt be a busy time.
Don't worry, we got you covered.

 
 

First day.


  • ☐

    Get an employee ID badge and parking sticker.

  • ☐

    Complete I-9 form.

  • ☐

    Request an RSA token by contacting the EIS service desk.

  • ☐

    Connect with your manager.

 
 

Within 7 days.


  • ☐

    Set up your work station.

  • ☐

    Set initial expectations with your manager, learn about the team, etc.

  • ☐

    Tour your work location.

  • ☐

    Update your personal info and tax withholding status in myHR.

 
 

Within 30 days.


  • ☐

    Enroll in Turner's health & wellness benefits (if applicable).

  • ☐

    Complete required compliance training (you'll be getting a lot of reminders).

  • ☐

    Set up 1:1 meetings with your manager/direct reports.

  • ☐

    Build your network (e.g., join a BRG).

 
 

31 days and beyond.


  • ☐

    Work with your manager on what training or on-the-job experiences you should focus on.
