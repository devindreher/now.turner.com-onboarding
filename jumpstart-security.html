ID Badges


  • ID badges must be worn at all times.
  • You may NOT share, loan, or borrow another employee's ID badge.
 

Visitors


  • All visitors are required to sign in with security.
  • To get a visitor badge in advance, visit Turner Now.
 

Medical Emergencies


  • Call 911 from an in-house phone. This will allow our Security Control Center to listen in and help with the emergency. If you cannot access an in-house phone, use your cell phone and then call Security (if possible).
 

Building Evacuations


  • Contact your floor warden at your work location for all building evacuation proedures.
 

Suspicious Packages


  • Report suspicious packages and activities to the Security Control Center for your work location.
 

Workplace Violence


  • Our policy on workplace violence is found on myHR. Turner has adopted a zero tolerance policy for workplace violence. Turner also expressively prohibits the carrying of firearms on our building premises.
