Turner prioritizes a balanced life where finances, health, and career are in sync.

Here's what Turner offers:

  • Medical, dental, and vision coverage
  • Spending accounts (health care, limited purpose, dependent day care, and health savings)

  • Optional group life insurance

Health Benefits.


If you remember nothing else from this page, remember this:

You have 30 days from your benefits eligibility date to make your benefits elections.

Visit timewarnerbenefits.com to review your benefit options and to make your elections.


Benefits eligibility:

  • Regular, full-time and part-time employees who are regularly scheduled to work at least 20 hours a week.
  • Benefits coverage begins the first day of the month following your date of hire.
  • If you're a rehire with a break in service of less than 30 days, your benefits coverage begins on your rehire date.
  • If you're converting from temporary to regular status, your benefits coverage may begin sooner than the first day of the month following your date of hire.

Contact myHR (VPN required) to verify your benefits eligibility date.

 
 

Medical, dental, and vision plans:

Plans available for benefits-eligible employees and eligible dependants which may include:

  • Your spouse or domestic partner
  • Your children and the children of your spouse or domestic partner, up to age 26

Refer to the summary plan descriptions at timewarnerbenefits.com for further information regarding eligibility.
