Health Benefits.


If you remember nothing else from this page, remember this:

You have 30 days from your benefits eligibility date to make your benefits elections.

Visit warnermediabenefits.com or call 1-800-690-1180 to review your benefit options and to make your elections.


Benefits eligibility:

  • Regular, full-time and part-time employees who are regularly scheduled to work at least 20 hours a week.
  • Benefits coverage begins the first day of the month following your date of hire.
  • If you're a rehire with a break in service of less than 30 days, your benefits coverage begins on your rehire date.
  • If you're converting from temporary to regular status, your benefits coverage may begin sooner than the first day of the month following your date of hire.

Contact myHR (VPN required) to verify your benefits eligibility date.

Employee Medical Contributions

  • You share the costs with bi-weekly pre-tax payroll contributions, which are based on salary band tier and coverage level:

    • Employee only
    • Employee + partner
    • Employee + child(ren)
    • Employee + partner + child(ren)
  • Medical, dental, and vision are separate pre-tax bi-weekly payroll contributions.
  • The cost of your medical, dental, and vision will be deducted from your paycheck retroactive to your benefits eligibility date.
 
 
 
 

401(k) Retirement Benefits

  • New, eligible employees are automatically enrolled at a contribution rate of 3% after two months of service, unless you opt out of automatic enrollment.
  • Contributions will be increased by 1% each year up to 6%.
  • The default investment option is the Growth Asset Allocation Fund.

Employee Contributions

  • Pre-tax payroll contributions: 2%-50%
  • 2018 IRS limits: $18,500; age 50 and over an additional $6,500

Employer Contributions/Company Match

  • The company match begins two months from your hire date.
  • Time Warner matches 7% on the first 6% contributed (133 1/3% on each dollar you contribute up to the first 3% and 100% on each dollar you contribute on the next 3% of eligible pay).
  • The company match is automatically allocated according to your investment elections.

Vesting

  • Vesting at 100% occurs after two years of service.
  • Any company contributions prior to July 1, 2010 will follow prior year vesting schedules.
