How to get your NT login


Create a network password at changepass.turner.com by completing the following info:


  • Username: type in your network username that was provided to you.
  • Old password: type in the default password Changemen0w.
    Note: This password is case sensitive.
  • New password: type in a new password of your choice using this criteria:
    • Between 8 and 16 characters.
    • Cannot contain your account name or parts of your full name that exceed 2 consecutive characters.
    • Must contain characters from 3 of the following categories:
      • English uppercase letters (A-Z)
      • English lowercase letters (a-z)
      • Base 10 digits (0-9)
      • Non-alphanumeric characters (e.g., ! $ # %)
  • Confirm password: type in your new password again.
  • Click submit when finished.
 
 
 
 

How to request a hard or soft RSA token


You have the option of having your RSA passcode (which gives you access to our network) on a key fob (i.e., a hard token) or on your mobile device (i.e., soft token).

  • Contact EIS/IT to request and receive a new token.
  • Once you receive the new token, go here. Log in using your network username and password.
  • After logging in, you will see a My Account page with your listed token(s) below.
  • Cick on set pin below your listed token.
  • Create a personalized pin. It must be 8 characters in length — all numbers, all letters, or alphanumeric; no special characters, repeating numbers, or sequential numbers are permitted.
 
 
 
 

Accessing Turner Wireless Networks


Turner employees worldwide needing wireless connectivity can connect their devices using the “tbsemployee” wireless network. For step-by-step instructions to connect your device to this network, visit mywirelessdevices.turner.com.

Turner guests and those traveling can may use the “WL-GUEST” and “twtraveler” wireless networks. Dial x9477 from an in-house phone to hear the GUEST wireless password for the day.

 
 
 
 

Email Retention & Document Management


Turner’s document management policy prescribes that employees should retain documents, including emails, only as long as they serve a business purpose. Therefore, all Turner employees should only retain email that is deemed business critical.

Emails in your inbox, sent items, and self-created email folders older than 60 days (from date sent/received) will be automatically deleted. To prevent business critical emails from automatic deletion and for more information, visit emailpolicy.turner.com.
