Our committment to diversity and inclusion.


We don't fear change... we encourage it.

Diversity and inclusion at Turner is authentic, distinctive voices both on and off the air. It's people who speak up, confront the challenge quo, and embrace change. And it's cultivating an environment that encourages every Turner employee to choose different.

Our goals:

  • Increase Turner's representation of women and people of color in overall leadership roles.
  • Increase diversity among writers, directors, product developers, and the creative teams behind our stories.
  • Expand our partnerships with diversity-focused non-profits and industry groups to boost their influence and expand our talent pipeline.
  • Incorporate independent rankings from recruiters, non-profits, and industry groups to better measure and benchmark our progress.

Learn more at turner.com/diversity.

 
 

Get involved, meet new people, and stay connected.


Turner's nine Business Resource Groups (BRGs) allow employees opportunities to connect with colleagues who share similar interests and backgrounds. These groups help foster professional development, promote diverse thinking, and connect our brands with our fans in more authentic ways.
